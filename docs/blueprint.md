# **App Name**: Crofton Concierge Care

## Core Features:

- Homepage Design: Homepage with a clean design, showcasing Dr. Williams and welcoming patients.
- About Dr. Williams: About page detailing Dr. Williams's experience, philosophy, and connection with MDVIP.
- Concierge Care Explanation: Dedicated section explaining the benefits of concierge care and the MDVIP partnership.
- Patient Testimonials: Testimonials section with a rotating carousel of patient experiences and reviews.
- Contact Information: Contact page with a secure contact form and clear directions.
- MDVIP Enrollment CTAs: Clear Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons to guide potential members to the MDVIP enrollment page.
- Google Maps Integration: Map to help patients locate the clinic.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A natural, calm green (#2E7D32), reflecting health and trustworthiness.
- Background color: Off-white (#F8F8F8), providing a clean, modern, and professional look.
- Accent color: A warm, inviting analogous green (#60A865), complementing the primary color to highlight key information and CTAs.
- Body font: 'Roboto', a clean, modern sans-serif for readability and professional feel.
- Headline font: 'Montserrat', a bold, geometric sans-serif to convey confidence and modernity.
- Use minimalist icons to represent services and information clearly.
- Modern, patient-first layout with clear sections and easy navigation.
- Subtle animations, such as smooth scrolling and fading transitions, for enhanced user experience.
